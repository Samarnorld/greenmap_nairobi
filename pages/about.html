About GreenMap

GreenMap is an environmental intelligence platform aimed at equipping communities, policymakers, and researchers with actionable knowledge regarding Nairobi's ecological health.

Our Mission

To empower communities and decision-makers with actionable environmental insights by democratizing access to vegetation, urban heat, and citizen-reported data - transforming science into community-driven solutions.

Our Impact

With GreenMap, Nairobians can visualize ecological threats before they escalate. Our platform helps track urban heat islands, inform afforestation efforts, monitor policy effectiveness, and promote sustainable urban design. We believe visibility is the first step toward accountability and change.
